Empty rows mean you haven't started logging yet; add a new row each time you work this folder, and note which product (by name) the row applies to once you have more than one live.

## Weekly review

Answer these 5 questions every week, in this file, using that week's rows above:

1. **Page views this week, per product?** (Pull from Gumroad's built-in analytics tab for each live listing — views, not sales, so you can see whether a distribution push actually drove traffic.)
2. **Conversion rate this week, per product?** (Sales divided by views for the week, per product, straight from Gumroad's dashboard — this tells you whether a low-revenue week is a traffic problem or a listing/pricing problem.)
3. **Revenue per product this week?** (Exact dollar figure per live product, not a combined total — a bundle or top seller can otherwise hide that several of your products are earning nothing.)
4. **Email subscribers gained this week?** (New follows via Gumroad's follow feature on any free/lite product, plus any list growth from distribution posts that linked straight to a signup.)
5. **Revenue vs. the $50-500/mo target — what's the plan for next week?** (State this month's running total, whether it's tracking toward the low or high end of that range at current pace, and one concrete next step — another distribution push, a new product, a bundle, a price change, or applying the README's kill criteria if a product line is at or past its 8-week/4-push mark with under $20.)
