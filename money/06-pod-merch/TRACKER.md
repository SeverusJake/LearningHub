## Weekly review

Answer these 5 questions every week, in this file, using that week's rows above:

1. **Designs live this week?** New designs actually uploaded and live, counted per platform (Redbubble and TeePublic separately), plus a running total live across both.
2. **Views this week?** Pull from each platform's own stats/analytics dashboard — total design views, per platform.
3. **Favorites this week?** Total "favorite"/"like" actions on your designs — a leading indicator of what's resonating before sales show up.
4. **Sales and royalties this week?** Count of items sold and the actual royalty dollar amount paid or pending, per platform — this is what counts toward the $20-300/mo ceiling, not views or favorites.
5. **Any trademark-check flags to note?** Any phrase that came back ambiguous or borderline in TESS or in a platform's IP policy, any platform warning or takedown received, and what you did about it. Check the trademark-check log above before answering — a clean "no flags" only counts if the log backs it up.
