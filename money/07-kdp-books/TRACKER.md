## Weekly review

Answer these 5 questions every week, in this file, using that week's rows above:

1. **Books live?** Count of titles currently live and for sale, ebook and paperback counted separately, plus a running total across the catalog.
2. **KENP reads + sales per book this week?** Pull from KDP's own Reports dashboard (kdp.amazon.com/reports) — Kindle Unlimited page reads (KENP) and unit sales, broken out per title, not just a catalog-wide total.
3. **Which niche is outperforming?** Compare this week's per-book numbers against the niche scoreboard in PLAYBOOK.md, and decide whether that changes next weekend's niche pick.
4. **Revenue vs. the $50-500/mo target?** This week's and the running month's revenue against the ceiling range — behind, on track, or ahead, and by how much.
5. **Next book's niche decided?** Yes or no, and which niche. If no, that's next session's first task, not something to figure out later.
