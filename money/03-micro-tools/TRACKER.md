## Weekly review

Answer these 5 questions every week, in this file, using that week's rows above:

1. **Visits this week?** (Total unique visits to the tool's landing page, per tool if running more than one — pull this from Cloudflare Web Analytics or whatever free analytics you wired up.)
2. **Signups/trials this week?** (Anyone who tried the free tier, created an account, or generated a license-key trial — whatever "started using it" means for that specific tool.)
3. **Purchases this week?** (Count of paid conversions, one-time or new subscriptions, plus the dollar total — this is what actually counts toward revenue, not visits or signups.)
4. **Feedback items shipped this week?** (How many bug fixes or feature requests from real user feedback actually went live, per Phase 2's "ship 1 improvement" rule.)
5. **Revenue vs. the $50-500/mo per-tool target — what's the plan for next week?** (State this week's revenue for each active tool, whether it's tracking toward the low or high end of that range at current pace, and one concrete change for next week — another launch attempt, a new SEO page, a pricing tweak, or applying the README's kill criteria if a tool is at or past its 6-week/3-launch mark with under 100 visits and $0 revenue.)
