# Money Track

A menu of AI-leveraged income experiments, built for an advanced IT learner working the global English-speaking market, getting paid via PayPal, starting with a $50 budget, and available for heavy weekend bursts rather than steady daily hours.

## Honest framing

No income here is guaranteed, and treating any single folder as "the plan" is the fastest way to waste your weekends. Run this track as a portfolio: launch several experiments, kill the ones that aren't converging within their checkpoint, and put your time into whichever ones show real signal. Most of the eight experiments below will underperform or flatly fail — that's normal, not a sign you did something wrong. Realistic ranges: month 1 is commonly $0-300 total across everything you try, month 3 is $100-1000 IF two or three experiments actually hit. Some learners will clear these numbers, some will spend a month and make nothing. Both outcomes are consistent with how this works. This is achievable, but it is variable — the work is running experiments, not executing a guaranteed formula.

## Cadence

- **Daily-loop friendly** (short feedback cycles, fits into small daily sessions): 01, 02
- **Burst/batch friendly** (produce a batch over a weekend, then let it sit and sell): 05, 06, 07
- **Compounding over time** (value builds slowly, payoff is back-loaded): 03, 04, 08

## $50 budget allocation

- ~$20: two domains — one for the 02 (biz-websites) portfolio demo, one for the 03 (micro-tools) product
- $30: reserve, held back for whichever experiment shows traction first (upgrading a Lemon Squeezy plan, a KDP proof copy, ad tests, etc.)
- Everything else (accounts, uploads, gig listings, Redbubble/TeePublic, Gumroad, Adobe Stock contributor signup) starts free.

## Compliance floor

Applies to every folder in this track, no exceptions:

- No fake reviews.
- No account misrepresentation or sharing.
- No spam.
- No undisclosed AI where a platform requires disclosure (Amazon KDP, Adobe Stock).
- No trademark/IP infringement.
- No fabricated credentials.
- Income figures are ranges with no guarantee.
- Taxes are the learner's own responsibility (this is not tax advice).

## Operating system

- Run at most 2 experiments at once. More than that and none of them get the attention needed to tell signal from noise.
- 4-week checkpoint against each folder's kill/scale criteria — every experiment gets checked at the 4-week mark against the specific bar set in its own folder, and it either gets scaled, held, or killed.
- 30-minute weekly review ritual: once a week, spend 30 minutes updating each active folder's `TRACKER.md` — numbers in, decision made, next action written down. Skipping this is how dead experiments quietly keep eating your weekends.
