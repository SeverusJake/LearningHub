## Weekly review

Answer these 5 questions every week, in this file, using that week's rows above:

1. **Proposals sent this week?** (Total Upwork proposals sent, all 3 niches combined.)
2. **Response rate?** (Of those proposals, how many got any reply from a client — as a fraction and a percentage.)
3. **Orders closed this week?** (Fiverr orders delivered + Upwork contracts won, combined count.)
4. **Avg $/order this week?** (Total revenue this week divided by orders closed this week.)
5. **Revenue vs. the $300-2000/mo target — what's the plan for next week?** (State this week's revenue, whether it's tracking toward the low or high end of the monthly range at current pace, and one concrete change for next week — e.g. raise proposal volume, drop a non-converting niche, raise prices per the scale criteria, or apply the 4-week kill criteria to a dead niche.)
