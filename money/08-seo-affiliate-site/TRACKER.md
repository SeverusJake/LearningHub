## Weekly review

**This folder runs on a monthly-granularity mindset even though you log weekly — don't expect week-to-week movement, and don't panic when a given week shows nothing.** Answer these 5 questions every week, in this file, using that week's rows above:

1. **Posts published this week?** Count, and running total against the 30-post cluster map (5 pillar + 25 supporting).
2. **GSC impressions/clicks trend?** Pull the trailing-4-week view from Google Search Console's Performance report — is the line moving up, flat, or down? One week alone is noise; the trend across several weeks is the signal.
3. **Affiliate link clicks?** Outbound clicks on affiliate links this week (from your site analytics or link-tracking, if set up) — this is your leading indicator before actual affiliate revenue shows up, since Amazon and most programs report sales/commissions on a delay.
4. **Revenue this month so far?** Pull from whichever affiliate dashboard(s) are active (Amazon Associates, or your specialized program) — remember payments lag behind clicks due to the cookie window and each program's own reporting delay.
5. **Month 1-3 wait-it-out phase, or month 4+ assessment phase?** State which phase you're in plainly. If month 1-3: keep publishing on schedule, do not judge the site yet. If month 4+: check this week's numbers against README.md's kill/scale criteria (30+ posts and a real upward impressions trend by month 4; kill at month 6 if trend is under ~500 impressions/day; scale the winning cluster if one is clearly outperforming).
